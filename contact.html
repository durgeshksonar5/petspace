Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I create an account on TailO?
Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Login Here" button at the top right corner and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide some basic information about yourself and your pets.
How can I list my pet services on Petspace?
After creating an account, go to your dashboard and click on "Add Service". Fill in the details about your service, add photos, and set your availability. Our team will review your listing before it goes live.
What areas do you currently serve?
We currently operate in Pune and surrounding areas. We're expanding rapidly, so if your city isn't listed yet, please contact us to express interest and we'll notify you when we launch in your area.
How does TailO ensure the quality of service providers?
We carefully vet all service providers on our platform. They must provide necessary certifications, undergo background checks, and maintain high ratings from our community. We also have a review system to help maintain quality standards.
